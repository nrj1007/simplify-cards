## User Preferences And Scope Rules

- Product is for Indian credit-card users.
- Official issuer pages remain the source of truth.
- Community/forum pages are signals only and should not directly rewrite card data.
- Discontinued and legacy cards should stay out unless explicitly requested.
- Cost should stay low, so no database yet.
- Approval workflows matter for scraped/community updates.
