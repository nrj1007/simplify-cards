## Data Entry Notes

For Axis cards, official pages often split information across:

- `/features-benefits`
- `/fees-and-charges`
- `/more-benefits`
- the new catalogue domain `axis.bank.in/cards/credit-card`
- older detail pages under `axisbank.com/retail/cards/credit-card/...`

Use `verificationStatus` like this:

- `official-direct` when one official Axis product page clearly contains the major facts.
- `official-mixed` when benefits, fees and terms are split across multiple official Axis pages.
- `official-catalogue` when only the catalogue provides enough public information and no detail page is cleanly available.

Be careful with Axis fees:

- Some cards are currently listed as nil-fee or lifetime-free in the catalogue while older detail fee tables still show standard fees.
- Preserve this nuance in `additionalBenefits` instead of silently flattening it.

Be careful with Axis reward rates:

- EDGE REWARD Points were stored as approximate statement-value equivalents using `statementBalanceValue: 0.2` where applicable.
- Merchant discounts and vouchers were usually captured in `additionalBenefits`, not as numeric rewards, unless a stable earn rate was obvious.
