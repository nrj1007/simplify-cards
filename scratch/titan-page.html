Good fit if: you want a card built for titan, jewellery, and watches, and you want your rewards as simple, no-tracking cashback.

Why it can work

It combines complimentary airport lounge access, accelerated rewards on world of titan, taneira, titan eye+, helios, fastrack, skinn, irth, and sonata, and milestone rewards.

The renewal fee is waived only on Rs 3 lakh of annual spend. Fuel don't earn rewards. The 3.5% forex markup is high for overseas use.
