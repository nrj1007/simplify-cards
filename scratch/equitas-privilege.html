Privilege Program

Privilege Program is a spend based milestone program where customers will be offered bouquet of benefits/services. All Equitas credit cardholders are eligible for tier-based benefits under the Privilege Program, applicable to all credit card variants—Tiga, Selfe, and PowerMiles. The program consists of five tiers: Blue (Base Tier), Silver, Gold, Platinum, and Diamond (Top Tier). To qualify for a tier, a user must spend a specified amount per month for three consecutive months. Silver starts at ₹20K per month, Gold at ₹40K, Platinum at ₹60K, and Diamond at ₹100K per month for three consecutive months in the calendar quarter (April to June, July to September, October to December and January to March)

By default, all cards will start with the Blue tier.

Customers will be upgraded or downgraded quarterly based on their spending thresholds within a calendar quarter.
